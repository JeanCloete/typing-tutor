What is Touch Typing?

Touch typing is a method of typing without using the sense of sight to find the keys. Instead, you rely on muscle memory, proper finger placement, and consistent practice.

Finger Placement

Your fingers rest on the "home row": ASDF (left hand) and JKL; (right hand). This is the foundation of all touch typing.

Accuracy First

Speed comes with time. Focus on typing correctly first — accuracy builds confidence and reduces errors.

Build Speed Gradually

As your accuracy improves, your WPM (Words Per Minute) will naturally increase. Our lessons guide you step by step.

Muscle Memory

With consistent practice, your fingers will "remember" where each key is — no more hunting and pecking!
